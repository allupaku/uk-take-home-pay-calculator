About This Tool

This calculator is a personal side project. It has no advertising, no profit motive, and no data collection by the author.

Your inputs are stored only in this browser session so the session key can reload them. The page does not submit your salary, benefits, tax, pension, RSU, or ESPP inputs to a server.

All calculations are estimates for personal planning only. They are not legal, tax, financial, pension, payroll, or employment advice, and they should not be treated as legally authoritative. Verify important figures with payroll, HMRC guidance, or a qualified adviser. The author accepts no legal responsibility for use of this tool or decisions made from it.
